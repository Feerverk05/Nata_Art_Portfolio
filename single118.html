Friends forever

Summer, village, sunset, the smell of mowed grass, the sounds of crickets in the grass, talking about everything in the world .. As if it was recently and so long ago .. These memories are with me forever, warm me and heal my soul. I dedicate this painting to my older brother, who is now protecting our country.
